Orange is a free and open source Jekyll theme with a minimal approach

Orange is built with the following modules worth a mention:

Portfolio

The portfolio module allows display of works in an elegant and responsive form.

A wide variety of content can be added and styled in the module's template to fit your use. See samples

Contact

The contact form module has built-in form validation using HTML5 with a JavaScript fallback and enhanced validation.

The form has a completed label when the form is submitted successfully.
